Help & Support

Welcome to the help page of Fitnessy. Here you can find answers to common questions and guidelines to help you get the most out of our platform. If you can't find the answer to your query here, feel free to contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I create an account on Fitnessy?

To create an account, simply visit the registration page and fill in your personal details, including your name, email, and password. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

2. How do I track my workouts?

Once you log in, go to the "Workout" section of your dashboard. You can input the details of your workout such as type, duration, and calories burnt. All your workout history will be stored for easy access.

3. Can I get fitness advice from professionals?

Yes! Fitnessy allows you to connect with certified fitness professionals. You can ask for personalized fitness advice and workouts to help you achieve your fitness goals.

4. How do I change my profile details?

To update your profile, go to the "Profile" section in your account. You can change your name, email, password, and other personal details there.

5. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be asked to enter your email, and a password reset link will be sent to you.
